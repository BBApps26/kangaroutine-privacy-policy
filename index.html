Introduction

Kanga-Routine is a math learning app for students. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Kanga-Routine collects and stores the following information:

  • Profile Information: Your display name and grade level
  • Game Progress: Your ELO ratings, statistics, and learning progress
  • Leaderboard Data: Your display name, weekly XP, and total ELO for the public leaderboard
  • Usage Data: Questions answered, accuracy rates, and streak information

How We Use Your Information

  • To save your progress and personalize your learning experience
  • To display your ranking on the weekly leaderboard
  • To track your streak and achievements
  • All data processing happens within the app and your iCloud account

Data Storage and Security

  • Private Data: Your profile, stats, and progress are stored in your private iCloud account. Only you can access this data.
  • Public Data: Your display name and scores appear on the public leaderboard, visible to all app users.
  • Local Storage: Some data is cached on your device for offline access.
  • We use Apple's CloudKit service, which is secured by Apple's privacy and security standards.

Data Sharing

  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We do NOT share your data with advertisers
  • We do NOT use tracking or analytics services
  • Leaderboard data (display name and scores) is publicly visible to other users

Children's Privacy

Kanga-Routine is designed for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information beyond what is necessary for the app to function. Parents and guardians can review and delete their child's data at any time by accessing the app.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: View all your data in the app's Stats and Settings screens
  • Modify: Change your display name and preferences in Settings
  • Delete: Delete your data by uninstalling the app. This removes all local data. iCloud data can be deleted through your iCloud settings.
  • Opt-out of Leaderboard: You can choose not to participate in ranked mode

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
