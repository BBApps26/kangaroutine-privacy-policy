Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my display name?
Go to the Home tab, tap the Settings button (gear icon), enter a new display name, and tap Save.
How does the leaderboard work?
The leaderboard resets every week (Monday) and ranks players by Weekly XP earned. You can view either "Everyone" or just players in your grade level.
What is ELO and how does it work?
ELO is a rating system that measures your skill level in each topic. You start at 1200. When you answer questions correctly, your ELO goes up. The harder the question, the more ELO you can gain!
How do I maintain my streak?
Play at least one game session (any mode) each day. If you miss a day, your streak resets to 1.
What's the difference between the game modes?
Practice: Unlimited questions and hearts. Great for learning!
Daily: 10 questions with 3 hearts. Counts toward your streak.
Ranked: 10 questions, unlimited hearts. Earns Weekly XP for the leaderboard.
My progress isn't syncing between devices. What should I do?
Make sure you're signed into the same iCloud account on both devices and have iCloud enabled for the app. Go to Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud and ensure Kanga-Routine is toggled on.
How do I delete my data?
Delete the app from your device to remove local data. To delete iCloud data, go to Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Manage Storage ‚Üí Kanga-Routine ‚Üí Delete Data.
Can I change my grade level?
Yes! Go to Settings and use the Grade stepper to select your current grade (1-12).
I found a bug or have a suggestion. How can I share it?
We'd love to hear from you! Email us at the address above with details about the issue or your suggestion.
Is this app suitable for my child?
Yes! Kanga-Routine is designed for students in grades 1-12. Content is educational and age-appropriate. See our Privacy Policy for information on data collection.
